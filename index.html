ABOUT US

Greetings! Lileli is a comic created by you for you. Our goal is to use comics and open talks to raise awareness and deconstruct abuse/assault culture, depression, anxiety, and self-esteem in young adults one step at a time.

Join our community today and begin reading what other people have to say, or start your own topic. Don't forget to use the hashtag #jointheconversation to share your story or experience. The stories submitted are anonymous.
