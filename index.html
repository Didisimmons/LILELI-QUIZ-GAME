ABOUT US

Hello there! Lileli is a comic made for you by you. Our aim is to raise awareness & deconstruct abuse/assault culture, depression, anxiety, and self esteem in young adults one step at a time through the use of comics & open conversations.

Join our community today and start reading what other users are sharing, or start your own discussion today.Feel free to take our quiz to know more about the abuse culture. If you have any questions, please get in touch. #jointheconversation.
